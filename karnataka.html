Temples
Mysuru Sri Chamundeshwari Temple

Perched atop Chamundi Hills in Mysuru, this temple is dedicated to Goddess Chamundeshwari, the patron deity of the Mysuru royal family. The temple offers panoramic views of Mysuru city and is famous for its golden idol of the goddess.

Udupi Sri Krishna Temple

This famous temple in Udupi is dedicated to Lord Krishna and is known for its unique idol installed by saint Madhvacharya. The temple follows strict traditions and is one of the most important pilgrimage sites in Karnataka.

Murudeshwar Shiva Temple

Located on the Arabian Sea coast, this temple features the second tallest Shiva statue in the world. The temple complex offers breathtaking views of the sea and is known for its impressive architecture and serene atmosphere.

Gokarna Mahabaleshwar Temple

One of the seven sacred Mukti Sthalas in Karnataka, this temple houses the Atmalinga of Lord Shiva. The temple town of Gokarna is known for its pristine beaches and spiritual significance.

Belur Chennakeshava Temple

A masterpiece of Hoysala architecture, this 12th-century temple is dedicated to Lord Vishnu. The temple is renowned for its intricate carvings depicting scenes from Hindu epics and daily life.

Halebidu Hoysaleswara Temple

Another gem of Hoysala architecture, this Shiva temple is famous for its detailed sculptures and friezes. The temple walls are adorned with thousands of figures from Hindu mythology.

Shravanabelagola Gommateshwara Statue

This 57-foot tall monolithic statue of Lord Bahubali is one of the most important Jain pilgrimage sites. Located atop Vindhyagiri Hill, the statue is visible from miles away.

Dharmasthala Manjunatha Temple

This unique temple is managed by Jain administrators but worships a Shiva linga. Known for its philanthropic activities, the temple attracts devotees from all faiths.

Kukke Subramanya Temple

Horanadu Annapoorneshwari Temple
